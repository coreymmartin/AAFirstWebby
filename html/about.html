Who Is The Jokester?

Like most people, I've had my fair share of tough times. I was discouraged and exhausted by everyday struggles. It seemed impossible to grow up; so I stopped. I asked myself, "why are you so serious? just have fun!" And from that moment on I have been finding the lighter and silly side of the everyday mishaps. Finding a way to turn each situation into a comical event, and now I just cant stop smiling.
